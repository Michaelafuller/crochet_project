Ready to draw? This tool is designed to help you place an image of your intended project next to the Literally Canvas drawing tool

As you can see from my example, being able to crochet doesn't mean that I can draw. But that's okay! The objective is to break the object down into the most basic geometric shapes.

By drawing the object, you will begin to see the basic shapes, the pieces of the whole. You might struggle crocheting a eye, but anyone can make a circle!
